Lasagna

Description

This recipe is mama's italiano world famous lasagna recipe. Its a most tasty recipe you ever had.

Mama italiano uses fresh ingredients, all fresh herbs, all fresh tomatoes, even the veal is ground straight from a live animal.

Ingredients List

  • tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • onions
  • garlic
  • parsley
  • veal
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Chop the onions and sautee them till translucent
  3. Add the garlic and sautee till the onions start to brown
  4. Crush the tomatoes and add along with chopped parsley to pot, simmer for 1hr
  5. Add ground veal and cook on slow heat for 30mins
  6. Assemble the lasgna in alternating layers of meat sauce, lasagna sheet, cheese.
  7. Bake for 45mins
